FAQ.



At what time shall I check-in/check-out of the surf camp?
Check-in is from 4:00 pm
Check-out is until 10:00pm

If you plan to arrive earlier or leave later, please, tell us in advance.


How big are the classes? Do yo have surf lessons for my surfing level?
In our surf school, it is established that each surf teacher will have no more than 5 students in the class. Each class will have students with the same surfing level (beginner, intermediate or advanced). Course lessons have a maximum of 4 students to each instructor.


How can I book my surf lessons?
Just book using the form on our website. Alternatively you can book by e-mail (infosurfsagres@gmail.com), phone (+351 914 482 407- office) or directly in our sales office. Bookings must be at least one day in advance in order to guarantee your place in our surf lessons the next day. Please note that a 50% deposit of the full payment will be needed to confirm your booking. The deposit should be completed by bank transfer or in cash if booked in our office.


I have it, but I don't like to travel with my surfboard. Do you have board rentals?
We are equipped with a wide range of surfboards to fit each and everyone's surfing level, height and weight.


At what time do you open/close your sales office?
July to October: 9:00am - 7:00pm
November to June: 9:00am - 5:00pm
Lunch break is from 1:00pm to 2:00pm.


How to find us?
Our surf school (sales office and surf camp), is located in Sagres main road EN 268.
Once you enter the town, just keep going straight until you find our sales office on the right side of the road.
On the opposite side of the road you can see an insurance company office (TRANQUILIDADE) and a bakery (PÃO QUENTE).

You can also use the GPS coordinates to help you find us: N 37°00'36" W 8°56'38.


My lesson is booked. How do I get to the beach? Do I have to rent surfing gear (board and wetsuit?)
The transfer to the beach and back is included in the surf lessons cost as well as a suitable wetsuit and a surf board according to you surfing level.


Do you sell any 2nd hand equipment?
We do! Check out our 2nd hand equipment page.


If I plan to stay around Sagres some more time after check-out, can you keep my luggage?
In case you have a surf lesson booked on the check out day or if for some reason you plan to stay for longer in Sagres, you can keep your luggage in our facilities.


At what time is breakfast served?
Breakfast will be served from 8:00am to 10:00am. This time may vary, according to our surf lessons schedule.

Please, let us know in advance In case you have any food allergies.


Ok, I've just booked my surf package. How do I pay?
In order to pay your booking balance, you can either make a bank transfer or pay cash in our sales office. Payments via bank transfer have to be done at least 3 working days before your arrival. For surf camp guests, the cash payment will be done during the check-in. In case you arrive after the office's closing hours your payment will be processed on the next morning. For surf lessons, surf courses or surf rentals, the balance should be paid on the 1st day.
