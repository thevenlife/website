Surf and Stay Packages.


We've packaged up 7 nights of accommodatoin with 5 days of surf lessons or courses to make it easy to book the perfect surf trip.
We've packed in some added bonuses for those who stay with us for the whole week, so simply choose what type of lessons you're interested in and what type of room you'd like to stay in and we'll do the rest.
If you need to change or modify any of the package details, just get in touch and we can help to organise a solution that works for you.



Lesson Package



The lesson package includes 5 days of group lessons, with the room of your choice for 7 nights in our surf camp.
Breakfast is included each day and we' thrown in an additional surf skate lesson for free, so you can really dial in the techniques you learn at the beach.
Lessons will usually run Mon-Fri so we recommend arriving over the weekend to get settled and ready for the week.

Course Package



Everything that comes with the lesson package, also comes with the course package... with a few upgrades.
You're lessons will be in smaller groups, with a maximum of 4 students per instructor. We'll also be filming at the beach and providing video analysis twice throughout the week.
You still get your free surf skate lesson, and we've also added theory sessions to the program - to tune in on the finer points of a variety of surfing topics.



Find Out More
Whichever package you choose, you'll be staying in your choice of rooms on our surf camp.
The camp has plenty of space indoors and outdoors - whether you want to relax after a good days surf, fire up the BBQ, or grab a beer and catch up with the other guests.
There are 2 fully fitted kitchens inside, multiple lounge areas, and a dedicated movie/teaching room. Outside, there's plenty space for everyone to chill out or catch the epic sunset views over the Cabo de San Vincente from your very own backyard.

Group lessons for all levels.


Lessons + Accommodation.


Our group lessons run daily, with a maximum group size of 8 per instructor. The lessons last between 2h30m and 3 hours, split into two 1h30m sessions, whenever possible.
This includes theory, technique practice and time in the water, and are tailored to suit the ability/experience of the group.
As well as the lessons at the beach, there'll be an additional surf skate lesson during the week included in the package.

Surf Camp

Our surf courses are all about having fun and and progressing. We'll help you to focus in on the proper technique to improve your surfing. We'll take you to the best waves at the best times to really get the most out of the session and also help develop your understanding of how the waves work.


Group Lessons

One of the most useful learning aids is video analysis. Being able to see the points you need to focus on really helps develop an understanding and push improvement. We run Surf Courses over both 3 and 5 days, so we'll add an extra session of video for those staying with us for the full 5 days.


Surf Skate Sesions

Courses + Accommodation.

Throughout the week we'll have 2 video analysis sessions and help you to create a personal evaluation and development plan.
