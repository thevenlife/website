Since 2001.


Created in 2001, we are the first local Surf School operating in this area and also one of the founders of the AESCV - Associação de Escolas de Surf da Costa Vicentina (Algarve's Surf Schools Association in the Natural Park).
International Surf School is based in Sagres, a place privileged by mother-nature, with coastlines facing both South and West. That is the reason why the area is known for having the highest swell consistency in all Continental Europe.
We have a certification from:

- FPS-Federação Portuguesa de Surf (Portuguese Surfing Federation) - license n. 34
- Turismo de Portugal (Portuguese Tourism Institute) - license n. 53/2008
- Câmara Municipal de Vila do Bispo - license n. 3/2021
- Câmara Municipal de Aljezur - license n. 7
- Câmara Municipal de Lagos - license n. 7/2020
- Alojamento Local (local accommodation) - license n. 7376/AL
- ICNF - Instituto de Conservação da Natureza e Florestas (Nature Conservation Institute)
- Turismo de Natureza
- ASI (Academy of Surfing Instructors)
- Clean & Safe
