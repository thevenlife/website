Group Lessons.


Our group lessons run daily, with a maximum group size of 8 per instructor. The lessons last between 2h30m and 3 hours, split into two 1h30m sessions, whenever possible.
This includes theory, technique practice and time in the water, and are tailored to suit the ability/experience of the group.
All our surf teachers are certified and very experienced in teaching students from absolute beginners to advanced surfers. They are enthusiastic, patient, and will always try to give you individual feedback, according to your personal surfing skill.

All Levels

From beginner to advanced, we tailor each lesson to suit the groups surfing ability - and we'll make sure to match your ability to the rest of the group so that nobody gets left behind. Group lessons are a great way to get started and learn through the basic techniques needed to progress from catching your first bit of tumbling white water, to paddling out back and dropping onto the face of an unbroken green wave.


Technique

Our team of dedicated and experienced instructors aim to give you the core techniques so you can learn how to surf properly - whether you're learning how to catch your first white water wave, or carving out the perfect cutback. We believe there's much more to surfing than simply standing up on a board and our aim is to develop your technique and understanding to give a solid foundation and progression through your surfing career.


Progress

More progress leads to more fun and that's what we're trying to achieve. By giving you the knowledge and the skills to improve, we hope you'll keep on progressing, keep on surfing, and carry on enjoying the ocean and the waves.
