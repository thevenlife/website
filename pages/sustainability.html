Located just 3 minutes walk to the local beaches in the heart of Sagres,
the surf camp is the ideal place to stay.


Home from home.


We don't want to be the typical hostel.
Our rooms sleep a maximum of 3 people to give you the space you need to enjoy your holiday.
We have a range of packages to suit everyone, whether you simply want to stay with us and free surf around Sagres, take your first ever surf lessons, or join one of our in depth surf courses. Whichever you choose, we'll aim to give you the best possible base to create your own surf adventure, and here's some of the things that are included:

There's plenty of space to socialise with a heap of outside lounging areas to take in some post surf sun. Inside there are 2 fully equipped kitchens, 2 lounges, and a dedicated movie and video teaching room all for a maximum of 12 people - whether you want to chill with some Netflix, fire up the BBQ, or find a quiet spot to read a book, we've got you covered.

Good vibes!
Relax, chill out, and surf with like minded people.



Fresh & Local

We want to provide the most wholesome, healthy breakfasts that we can, whilst trying to remove plastic from the breakfast table and offer fresh and local produce wherever possible. We have fresh baked bread delivered each day, locally made cheese, and shop daily at the local market for the best seasonal fruit and veg. There's plenty to fuel you up for the days surf, and plenty of vegetarian and vegan friendly options too!


Art & Design

We've spent a lot of time renovating and improving the Surfcamp. From new artwork and board storage areas to our showcase corridor and handmade wall decorations. Whether it's your first visit or you've been coming for years, there's plenty of new things to see and be inspired by.


Explore

The little town at the end of the world has way more to offer than just it's beautiful coastline. Steeped in history and culture, this small fishing village is well worth exploring. The camp sits just a few minutes walk from the nearest beaches, the historic Fortaleza and the local harbour - and a stones throw away from a unique collection of bars and restaurants.
