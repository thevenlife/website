Beginner to advanced -
we have the lessons to help you progress.


Learn to surf.


Surfing is much more than just standing on the board.
You'll need not only the balance, but also the control. That can only be achieved with strong basic technical skills.
All our surf instructors are fully certified and very experienced in teaching students from absolute beginners to advanced surfers. The lessons are happening every day. Usually, we leave the surf school around 09:00am but, if needed, we might adapt the schedule to suit the best surf conditions available at the time. Most of the times the surf lessons take place on wild beaches with no infrastructure so don't forget your lunch box, some water, and sun protection.

At International Surf School we don't just help you stand up on a board - we teach you how to surf.
Whether it's a Group Lessons, to Private Lessons or Surf Courses, we have the right lesson to match your ability and improve your surfing.

Uniquely positioned with access to
both the West and South coast of Portugal.



Stunning Beaches

We have so many amazing beaches to choose from. Each day we assess the local conditions and forecasts to choose the spot for the lessons. With access to the South and West coast and consistent Atlantic swell all year, we aim to find the beach with the best conditions - to make sure you get the best waves.


Technique

Our lessons will give you the technique and understanding to really improve your surfing. Our teachers give personal feedback and tailor the lesson, demonstrations, and exercises to help you step up to the next level - whether it's the basics of the take-off or carving out the perfect cutback.


Explore

Sagres is an amazing part of the world, blessed with an equally amazing coastline. You'll get to see some of the more spectacular beaches and surrounding areas in the search for the perfect waves.

Surf, progress & have fun.




Group Lessons



Group lessons are the ideal start to your surfing journey, or to build more advacned techniques and skills. There's a maximum of 8 students to each instructor and we make sure to group you with other surfers of a similar level to make sure nobody gets left behind.

Private Lessons



If you really want to fast track and focus, a private lesson gives you one to one, undivided attention from your instructor. The lesson will be tailored specifically to your needs, whether you're just starting out or looking to perfect more advanced manoeuvres.



Find Out More


Surf Courses



Our surf courses last 3 or 5 days with maximum group sizes of 4 per instructor. We've added video analysis to the lessons and will help you to develop a personal evaluation and development plan to maximise your surf progression.
