Like alcohol, but for your ears


Since 2014.


Created in 2001, International Surf School is based in Sagres, a place privileged by mother-nature, with coastlines facing both South and West. That's the reason why the area is known for having the highest swell consistency in all Continental Europe - our lessons will give you the technique and understanding to really improve your surfing.
Our surf instructors give personal feedback and tailor the lesson, demonstrations and exercises to help you step up to the next level, whether it's the basics of the take-off or carving out the perfect cutback.
Our surf camp is located just a 3 minute walk from the beaches in Sagres and we don't want to be the typical hostel - our rooms sleep a maximum of 3 people and there are plenty of indoor and outdoor spaces to rest and recuperate ready for the next days surf.

Stunning Beaches

We have so many amazing beaches to choose from. Each day we assess the local conditions and forecasts to choose the spot for the lessons. With access to the South and West coast and consistent Atlantic swell all year, we aim to find the beach with the best conditions to make sure you get the best waves.
