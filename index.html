The Wine Club was born in the French mountains - took a brief spell in Greece - before returning to it's spiritual home in the Alps. Chris & Dave cut their teeth busking on the snowy streets of Morzine and perfoming regularly to packed houses at Cafe Chaud's open mic night, becoming a much celebrated warm up act to rouse the crowds before Monday's Punk Night.
